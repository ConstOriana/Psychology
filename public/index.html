About Me


I'm a Psychoterapist working from a Lacanian Psychoanalysis approach. I got my degree at Universidad Nacional de Córdoba, one of the biggest universities in Argentina. Since 2020, I've been providing individual online therapy to people across the world. I've also studied advanced English, which allowed me to work with both hispanic and English speaker consultants.

Since adolescence, the strong desire to delve into the human mind led me years later to apply what I had learned in the clinical field of Psychotherapy. Ongoing training and continuous study are key resources for working from an open and constantly evolving theoretical-practical approach.

Anxiety and Stress

___________________________

Anxiety and stress are common, often intertwined emotional experiences. Anxiety involves apprehension about future events, while stress is the body's response to demands or challenges. In modern life, these feelings can become overwhelming due to factors like uncertainty and overwhelm.

Social and Cultural Context

___________________________

Mental health is profoundly shaped by social and cultural context. Stigma, access to resources, and societal norms vary across cultures. Socioeconomic factors and discrimination can affect mental well-being. Recognizing these influences is vital for effective mental health support.

Sexuality

___________________________

Sexuality is a complex aspect of human identity encompassing sexual orientation, attraction, and gender identity. It's essential to respect and understand diverse expressions of sexuality. Promoting inclusivity and providing accurate information can foster a more accepting society.

Family

___________________________

Family is a fundamental influence on our psychological development. It provides support, love, and sometimes challenges. Healthy family dynamics can bolster mental well-being, while dysfunctional relationships may contribute to stress or mental health issues.

Reconstructing

___________________________

Identity is not fixed; it can be reconstructed throughout life. Major life events, self-discovery, or therapy can prompt identity shifts. Embracing and understanding this fluidity is a crucial aspect of personal growth and mental health.

Depression

___________________________

Depression is a prevalent mental health condition characterized by persistent feelings of sadness, fatigue, and a loss of interest in activities. It can have a profound impact on daily life, relationships, and physical well-being.
